Cardiovascular and Strength Standards for Norwegians

This is a table of all of the percentiles we were given from the start. From this table is where we generated our dataset to output percentiles and other key information.


The VO2 max score is a measurement of the maximum amount of oxygen a person can utilize during exercise. It is measured in milliliters of oxygen consumed in one minute, per kilogram of body weight. Our calculations used the Jack Daniels Equation which provides a close approximator for this value(Capritto, 2019).


The Maximal Treadmill Runtime was calculated through testing each participant, beginning with 6 minutes of walking at 5 kilometers per hour at a 10% incline. The speed was then increased by 1 km/hr every minute, still at a 10% incline (Aandstad, 2021).
