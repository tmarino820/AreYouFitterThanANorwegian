About This Data

The author of the article that is associated with this dataset is Anders Aandstad - a PhD. and researcher from the Norwegian School of Sports Science (Aandstad, 2021).

The article was published by the European Journal of Applied Physiology. However, the data was provided by the Norwegian Armed Forces HR and Conscription Centre (Aandstad 2021)

The original spreadsheet included many rows across strength and cardio exercises. However, we normalized the data by omitting all of the rows except: Treadmill run time (min:sec), and Est. VO 2peak (mL * kg^-1 *min^-1) from the Men and Women sections.

Our data was collected between 2011 and 2019 by conscript selection centers in Norway, and includes “154,659 subjects -- 66% men and 34% women, 17–21 years old -- who completed physical examinations at conscript selection” (Aandstad, 2021).

The Norwegian Armed Forces HR and Conscription Centre did not make the data in its raw format available to everyone for public viewing due to privacy concerns. However, Aandstad's paper releases general information about individuals over the range of performance levels. From this, we fit distribution curves and generated 10,000 “synthetic” Norwegians across percentiles.
